
Chat app strategy

A group consists of two or more persons
Each person can post in the group that they are a part of.
Individuals must sign up to create a user. 
Once a user has been created they can register for groups. Or create groups. 
Group creator will by default be the admin of the group
Can send out invites to members to join groups.
Individuals landing page/dashboard will display groups that they are a part off.
